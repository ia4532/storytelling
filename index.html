About 2 to 3 years ago, I was playing a game called "Dark Souls 2". It is a really frustrating game but it really does help kill time when you're really bored. One day, I had 400,000 souls. I didn't bothered using it and continued on with my adventure. I was excited when I discovered a new dungeon until I was being ambushed by enemies.

I was so focused on trying not to die while fighting back, I didnt pay attention to where I was backing up and fell into a hole. After I respawn back to the nearest bonfire, I was really determined to get my souls back. I carefully made my way back to the same dungeon and killed all the enemies just to see that my souls were sitting on a plank of wood inside the hole I fell in. So I gave myself a few moments before going in.

No matter how careful I was, I still manange to miss my landing and I permanently lost 400,000 souls when I could've used it to increase my stats. I didn't know if I should be mad about it or not. Even until now, I still don't know if I should be mad. All I know is that I learned my lesson when it comes to Dark Souls. Never be a hoarder in this game.

The End
